Reservations

Priceline

Kayak

The first step to a successful vacation is finding safe and reliable flights. Two such websites are Kayak and Priceline

Flights are labeled based on price. Typically its cheaper to make purchases a few weeks or months ahead of time so be sure to plan your trip around that.

Expedia

Cruises are often a great alternative for those who want more economic options or for those who potentially might be afraid of flying. Expedia often offers great rates

Do note that Carribean cruises are typically limited in their itinerary and port locations
